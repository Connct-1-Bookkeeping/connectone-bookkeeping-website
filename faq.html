Frequently asked questions

Helpful answers before we get started.

Still unsure? Contact us and we can discuss your bookkeeping requirements.

Do you work with businesses remotely?

Yes. We can provide cloud-based bookkeeping support to businesses across Australia using secure digital systems and cloud accounting platforms.

Which accounting software do you support?

We support Xero, QuickBooks Online and MYOB. The exact work performed depends on your agreed bookkeeping scope.

Can you prepare and lodge BAS?

Yes. BAS preparation and lodgement support is available through our registered BAS Agent services, subject to the agreed engagement and information being provided on time.

Do you provide BAS-only services?

Our preferred model is to provide BAS services as part of an ongoing bookkeeping engagement so the underlying records can be maintained and reviewed consistently. Please contact us to discuss your situation.

Do you provide payroll support?

Yes. We can assist with payroll processing, STP reporting, employee records and related payroll bookkeeping tasks.

What documents may you need from me?

Depending on the engagement, we may request bank statements, invoices, receipts, contracts, payroll information and other records relevant to the transactions being processed.

How quickly should I provide documents before a BAS deadline?

To allow adequate time for bookkeeping and review, provide requested information as early as possible. The specific document deadline will be confirmed as part of your engagement and workflow.

Can you help set up my accounting software?

Yes. We can assist with bookkeeping file setup, chart of accounts, bank feeds and practical bookkeeping workflows in supported software.

Do you offer fixed fees?

Fees are quoted based on the agreed scope, transaction volume, payroll requirements and business complexity. Your engagement letter will confirm the applicable fees and terms.

How do I get started?

Book a free initial consultation or send us an enquiry. We will discuss your business, software and bookkeeping needs before providing the next steps.
